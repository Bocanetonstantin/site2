Our Mandate

Redefining Industrial Horizons

Jacaranda Studio Sao Dimas SPE LTDA operates as a high-precision Special Purpose Entity (SPE) under the executive leadership of Key Principal Osmair Gozetto. Our mandate is to deliver structural integrity and operational efficiency in the industrial sector. By focusing on niche building construction and subdivision projects, we provide the infrastructure necessary for global commercial expansion.
